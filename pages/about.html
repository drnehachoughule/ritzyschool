About Our School

Building a strong foundation for the leaders of tomorrow.

Our Story

A decade of excellence in early childhood education.

Founded in 2010, Ritzy Junior School began with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a space where children don't just learn, but flourish. We believe that the early years are the most critical in a child's development.

Located in the serene surroundings of Bicholi Hapsi, Indore, our campus is designed to be a second home. We combine traditional values with modern Montessori and Play-way methods to ensure holistic growth.

ISO Certified

Recognized for Quality Education

Our Mission

To provide a safe, stimulating, and caring environment where every child is encouraged to explore their potential. We aim to foster independence, critical thinking, and a lifelong love for learning through innovative teaching practices.

Our Vision

To be the leading pre-school in Indore that sets the benchmark for early childhood education, creating global citizens who are compassionate, confident, and creative thinkers ready to embrace the future.
